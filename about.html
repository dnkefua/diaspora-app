About

A home for the
global diaspora.

The Diaspora App is a curated, premium discovery platform connecting members of the global diaspora to authentic, vetted businesses and events run by their own community — wherever life takes them.

Mission

Make the diaspora
findable to itself.

From the best Nigerian rice in Deira to the sharpest barber in Al Karama, the most-booked braider in Toronto to the most-loved photographer in London — we bring the diaspora's finest businesses out of group chats and into one curated home.

Promise

Premium, not
pushy.

Discovery should feel like a magazine, not a coupon site. Every listing, every advertisement, every event is presented with care. Sellers keep 100% of revenue. Members keep their trust.

What we stand for

Five commitments, always.

Diaspora-owned onlyEvery listing is verified before it goes live.
Zero commissionDirect WhatsApp commerce. We never take a cut of a seller's earnings.

Operator

from Dubai.

About

Built for the diaspora,
by the diaspora.

A curated home for the diaspora's finest — authentic kitchens, trusted stylists, gifted photographers, and the jobs that keep our communities growing.

Our story

The Diaspora App began in Dubai with a simple frustration — the best Nigerian rice, the sharpest Ghanaian barbers, and the most gifted Kenyan photographers were scattered across group chats, neighborhoods, and rumor.

We built the home they deserved. A single curated marketplace where authenticity is verified, communities discover each other, and every business owner keeps 100% of what they earn. No middlemen. No commissions on your kitchen, your chair, or your craft.

What we believe

  • Discovery should feel premium. Every listing is treated like an editorial feature — full-bleed photography, clean typography, restraint over noise.
  • Sellers keep what they earn. We charge for placement, never for transactions. Your customers come direct.
  • Trust is non-negotiable. Verified businesses, real reviews, no pay-to-play in organic results.
  • Local, but global. A jollof spot in Deira and a braider in Peckham deserve the same shelf.

By the numbers

Live in 47 cities across 23 countries. Featured kitchens from Lagos to Toronto. Approved stylists from Jumeirah to Brixton. Verified photographers, tailors, and event teams across every diaspora hub we serve.

We are a small team building consumer products for under-served global communities. If you have shipped quality work and care about the craft, we want to hear from you.

Why here

  • Editorial bar. We treat product like a magazine — typography, photography, and restraint matter.
  • Owner economics. We don't take commissions from sellers. Our success is tied to making them money.
  • Distributed by default. HQ in Dubai, city editors worldwide. We hire where the talent is.
  • Small, senior team. Few meetings, lots of shipping, real ownership over the work.

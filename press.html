One-line pitch

The Diaspora App is a curated, commission-free marketplace where diaspora-owned restaurants, salons, photographers, and creative talent are discovered globally — built for the global diaspora, by the global diaspora.

Quick facts

  • Founded — 2026, Dubai (UAE)
  • Parent company — NDN Analytics
  • Markets — 47 cities, 23 countries
  • Categories — Restaurants, food sellers, hair stylists, barbers, photographers, tailors, beauty, jobs, events
  • Business model — Premium ad placements only. Zero commission on transactions.
  • Stack — Web (vanilla HTML/CSS/JS), React Native mobile, Firebase backend

Founder statement

"The best diaspora businesses live in group chats and rumor. We built the home they deserved — premium, commission-free, owned by the people who make it run."

— The Diaspora App founding team

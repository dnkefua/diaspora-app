The Diaspora App is a premium discovery and advertising platform for diaspora-owned businesses, services, and events worldwide. Operated by NDN Analytics from Dubai, the platform is mobile-first, mobile-web accessible, and runs on a zero-commission model — sellers keep 100% of their revenue, while the platform monetizes premium placements and subscriptions.

A tribute to the global diaspora soul — authentic food, skilled hands, and the jobs that keep our communities thriving.

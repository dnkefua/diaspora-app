How it works

  1. List your business — free, takes ~5 minutes. Upload photos, set your hours, add your WhatsApp.
  2. Choose a placement — pick a tier above, pick your week, send us your hero photo.

Frequently asked

Do you take a commission on sales?

No. We charge for placement only. When a customer taps through, they go straight to your WhatsApp, phone, or profile — we are never in the middle.

Can I run on multiple cities?

Yes. Multi-city campaigns get 15% off the second city, 25% off three or more.

What does "editorial fit" mean?

Every paid tile is reviewed for image quality before it ships. We will work with you on the shot if needed — premium photography is the whole point. We may also offer in-house photography in your city for an additional fee.

How do I pay?

Card or bank transfer in your local currency. Invoiced weekly or monthly. Annual contracts get 10% off.
